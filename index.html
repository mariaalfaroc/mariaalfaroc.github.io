María Alfaro-Contreras

Computer Science PhD Student | FPU Predoctoral Researcher

Hi!

I am a FPU Predoctoral Researcher at the Software and Computing Systems Department of the University of Alicante, doing my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Jorge Calvo-Zaragoza and Prof. Jose M. Iñesta.
I am part of the MultiScore project. Right now, my research focuses on applying (multimodal) machine learning techniques for audio and image music transcription, in other words, I am trying to teach computers how to read music. It is being a fun experience!

As you might figure out, I like music. I also enjoy learning new things and spending time in nature. I am always up for a chat, so feel free to drop me an email!
